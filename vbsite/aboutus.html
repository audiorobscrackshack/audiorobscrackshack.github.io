PROP SHOP BIO'S



Robert Moseley
aka: "Videobob



Bob is the "Bob" in "Audio Rob's Crack Shack".
Known for his red suspenders and signature hat adorned with spikes, his passion for his favorite films is the fuel for the shop's projects.

Robert "Videobob" Moseley got his nickname as a video producer making music videos, TV shows and commercials.
Videobob works with close friend, Vinnie Paul Abbot, famed drummer of the heavy metal band "PANTERA" and his new project, "HELLYEAH".
Bob has produced music videos and DVD's for the band and Vinnie's record label "BIG VIN RECORDS".
Videobob's videos can be found in retail outlets such as BEST BUY, FYE, etc. and seen on MTV2, FUSE and other music video channels.
Videobob has also been a television personality and host of some of his own shows as well as acted bit parts in national television series.
Bob's shop has also built props used in major motion pictures.

An avid Delorean enthusiast, Videobob began to convert his own Delorean into a Time Machine back in 2004.
The quest to build the car and get it perfect has taken over 8 years and many trips to California to do research on the original cars.
Since buying his first Delorean in 2003, Bob has become a bit of an expert in the Delorean sports car owing a dozen Deloreans to date
and is well known to the community and thousands of  Delorean owners and fans.

Sorry ladies,
Bob is married to his lovely wife Rachel and they live in South Dallas, Texas.


Omar Carrillo


Omar is our shop forman.
He is in charge of many of the props that we make and our head Ghostbuster mechanic, he does all the sanding and finishing to our resin parts
and makes sure that quality control is assured.
Aside from being a fiberglass and resin expert, Omar is a talented guitarist, drummer and full time metal head!
He practices yoga and martial arts, web design and art.
You will see him working on webcam almost anytime! The dude never stops!!!



Jack Driver


Jack is our head mechanic, paint and body man.
He is a former taxi / limo / commercial truck driver with a CDL and vast knowledge of traffic and gun laws. It's scary.
An avid enthusiest of weird cars such as the Checker Marathon, Bradley GT and odd limousines made from unlikely vehichles.
His dream car is the "Eleanor" Mustang, but he loves to work on most any car.
He has become an expert in the restoration of the Delorean and adapted at painting old Cadillacs.
If it rolled out of our shop, he helped make that possible.
