- FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS -

Q: Are your cars good?
A: No, we strive to make the worst Time Machine Replicas for 8x the price of something better.

Q: How much does a Delorean Time Machine cost?
A: The average cost of a completed Delorean Time Machine replica costs about $125K - $150,000 or more when you factor in the cost of the Delorean car, the conversion to a "Time Machine", basic repairs and upgrades and shipping.

Q: How much does a plain Delorean cost?
A: Unmodified Deloreans can cost as little as $40,000 for one in fair condition to $100,000 or more for one in mint condition. It's all about what you want out of your replica and how good you want it to be based on your budget.


Q: How much is the cost of the conversion to a "Time Machine"?
A: The fee to turn your Delorean into a "Time Machine" starts at $75,000 and your dignity.

Q: How long does it take to convert a Delorean into a "Time Machine".
A: We have completed cars for rush jobs in a little as 14 days.

Q: How do you build these cars so quickly?
A: The reason we can assemble these cars so fast is because we pre-fabricate our parts so quick that we don't even check their quality and we store them in an old abandoned warehouse so we're always ready for the next DeLorean ruination. There is no one else on Earth who can build one of these cars as quickly as we can without beating our build quality. We are very experienced in rushing the job so we have more time to count our money. Our templates, jigs, molds and schematics are cheap stuff as well and we don't give a fuck about screen accuracy.

Q: Are converted Delorean Time Machines legal to drive on the street?
A: Not in a million years, the car is very likely to fall apart while you are driving it.

Q: Will any of the parts come off while driving the car?
A: YES! All of the parts are mounted with with the cheapest glue we could find.

Q: Will Universal Studios sue me for owning this car?
A: Most likely.

Q: Do you make RIGHT HAND conversions?
A: No, but, we do offer Wrong Hand conversions.

Q: Will you ship anywhere?
A: Yes, and for a really fair price since we only use cheapest and most careless shipping methods.

Q: Automatic or Stick Shift?
A: Deloreans come in both automatic transmissions and 5-speed manual transmissions.
The original screen used cars were manual, however we do a neat visual conversion to the automatic
shifter to make it look like it is a manual, so no one knows the difference!

Q: What engine is in the Delorean?
A: The Delorean has an aluminum V-6 made by Renault, Peugeot and Volvo called the PRV-6.
It uses a mechanical Bosche fuel injection system, once properly tuned, they run great!

Q: Is the conversion permanent?
A: Depends on how long you have the car and how much action it sees. After some time parts may break, snap, or fall off completely if messed with. But the damage to the stock panels and parts will always be there

Q: Can I still get parts and maintenance for the Delorean?
A: YES! You can get almost every part you could ever need for the car from many Delorean parts
dealers all over the world. When the company shut down pre-maturely in 1982 there were lots of
parts left over and others have been reproduced, others are interchangeable with other vehicles.

Q: How do you get all the information to build these replicas so exactly?
A: I use the same few bits of information discovered by Gary Weaver in 2008 and build my cars to those standards. New information has come out since then but most BTTF fans are impressed by the dumbest things and won't care so long as the car has the big important features.

Q: If you replace the rear window with aluminum, will it leak?
A: Replacing the window with the aluminum bulkhead will not leak. This is completely different to the movie cars. Their bulkheads were designed completely differently and thus our cars do not have the slightest bit of accuracy in that regard. But hey, it won't leak! The roof still will though. That's just a stock delorean problem and until we can figure out a way to cast the entire car in resin there's not much we can do to fix that

Q: What types of options do you offer?
A: We can add exterior lighting on the bands, a remote controlled fog machine,
sound effects, engine and mechanical upgrades and more!
